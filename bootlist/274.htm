3) Temporary Mouse Freeze

Elco inquired - PS/2 mouse sometimes get stuck for 1 or two seconds and then the cursor moves again, mostly to an unexpected place since the last movements of the mouse are 'remembered'.

Also by clicking, to start a program, open another window, the system 'freezes' for a second but then continues without problem. I tried other mouses, PS/2 and USB ; still the same phenomena.


*** I'd venture to say that 95% of the time that is due to the last application you have installed or set to run 24/7. For example, I often run a way old, outdated version of Winfax in the background when I need to receive faxes. Every time I do my mouse does the exact same thing. After my faxes are received I disable Winfax and all is back to normal.

As far as a fix? That would depend on the specific program causing the hang, which of course, is often spyware.
