1) Problems Installing A New Floppy Drive

Mr. Washington asks - I am having problems installing a floppy disk drive. After I connect up the cable and the power source and boot up the computer the indicator light on the floppy disk just stays on continuously. The is no sign of the drive in my computer. What could I be doing wrong?


*** The data cable is installed backwards. Or, you're putting it on a tad ajar and missing connecting two pins on either side of the cable. Note that 90% of the time pin one for the data cable is next to the power supply connector.

Also make sure it's set to a 1.44 in your bios as sometimes the cmos resets itself to NONE, which makes sense actually if you originally connected it incorrectly.

3) XP Problems With Secure Websites

pookster inquires - When I try to log onto [some webpages including my hotmail] its telling me its a secure site and wont load it. How can I make it load this [and other secure] site(s)?

Taurarian delineates:

In Internet Explorer
1. Tools | Internet Options | Advanced
2. Scroll down to the Security Settings
3. Make sure that the two boxes are checked that say "Enable SSL 2" or "Enable SSL 3" [Now] try your secure sites.

Another thing you can try is:
Go to start/run
Type in: regsvr32 softpub.dll
Press Enter or click OK

http://support.microsoft.com/?kbid=305217

5) Can I Remove SP2 From My Brand New PC?

PapaBear complains - Can anyone help me in removing service pack 2, as it is slowing down some applications. The service pack came already installed on the machine [I just bought] and if you go into add/remove programs it is not in there to remove.

Bruce C. says:
If Service Pack 2 was provided pre-installed when you purchased the computer, there's no way to uninstall it short of formatting the hard drive and performing a clean installation of the OS, using an installation CD that does not have SP2 slip-streamed in to its files.
