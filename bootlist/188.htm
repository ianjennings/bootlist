3) The Two Best Free Archive Managers

I've been using UltimateZip for ages, specifically version 2.5. Free, no ads or popups. I also like it because it handles a rather large number of types of archives including .tar, .gz, and .rar. Lately tho I've noticed it takes a rather long time to open up large archives of 100 or 200 files so I tried out the newest version 2.7.

http://www.ultimatezip.com/

2.7 has a sponsor window which briefly appears but it's still free and it's real fast with large archives so I still recommend it. In any case I searched around for more of the best and found TugZip which is also free and pure and perhaps even a bit faster with large archives. It's hard to choose which one is better, but in my opinion both are the best of the 30 or so free archive managers.

http://www.tugzip.com/
